Privacy Policy for ITEL Learn

Last updated: September 25, 2025

What We Collect

ITEL Learn is a learning management app that helps students access educational content. We collect the following information:

  • Camera Access: We access your camera to allow you to take photos for profile pictures and assignment submissions.
  • Photo Library Access: We access your photo library so you can select existing photos for profile pictures and assignments.
  • File Access: We access files on your device to allow you to upload documents, homework, and educational materials.
  • Audio Settings: We modify audio settings to play educational videos and audio content properly.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for educational purposes:

  • To enable you to submit assignments and homework
  • To set up and customize your learning profile
  • To provide access to educational content and materials
  • To facilitate communication with teachers and educational institutions

What We DON'T Do

  • We do NOT sell your personal data
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT access your data for commercial purposes

Data Storage and Security

Your uploaded files and photos are stored securely and are only accessible to you and your educational institution. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for educational use by students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without proper parental consent through educational institutions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
